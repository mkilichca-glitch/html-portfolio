Best and favorite movie franchise according to Kilichca

My top 5 movie franchises of all-time!

Harry Potter

A young orphan living with abusive gaurdians is swept away to a school of witchcraft and wizardy. Learn and unravel secrets of Harry's life through the dark mysteries of Hogwarts.

The Hobbit

Follow the amazing adventures of Bilbo Baggings accompanied by 13 dwarves to reclaim stolen treasure. Will the treasure be used for good or for evil?

Pirates of the Caribbean

In the golden age of piracy, find a lucked-up loony pirate named Jack accompanied by his crew of frenemies. Sail with them on their supernatural and swashbuckling adventures!

The Matrix

Trapped in a simulated reality and enslaved by sentient machines, watch how Neo starts to asks the right questions to break through the Matrix. Will it be worth it?

The Ice Age

In a changing world, follow an unlikely "herd" of pre-hestoric mammals as they navigate through these changes with adventure, fun and laughter.

Grap some popcorn and Astros, mix it up, sit back and enjoy the list of my favorite movie franchises!
